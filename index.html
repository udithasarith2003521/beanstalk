COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS

What types of coffee do you serve?

We serve a variety of coffee options including espresso, lattes, cappuccinos, Americanos, macchiatos, mochas, and cold brews. We also offer brewed coffee in different flavors and blends.

Do you offer non-dairy milk options?

Yes, we offer a selection of non-dairy milk alternatives such as almond milk, oat milk, soy milk, and coconut milk

Do you have decaffeinated coffee?

Yes, we offer decaffeinated options in both brewed coffee and espresso drinks.

What food items do you serve with coffee?

We serve a variety of freshly baked pastries, sandwiches, wraps, and snacks such as muffins, croissants, cookies, and granola bars.

Can I customize my drink (e.g., extra shots, flavors, sweetness)?

Absolutely! Feel free to customize your drink by adding extra shots, flavored syrups, adjusting sweetness levels, or choosing your preferred milk.
